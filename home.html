What is Beat the Backlog?

Beat the Backlog helps gamers manage their personal collections by organizing games into categories, tracking completion status, and recording ratings.

Track your personal game collection

Games can be categorized as Playing, Completed, or Backlog. The user dashboard summarizes progress and displays useful gameplay statistics.

Discover Your Next Adventure

The application integrates the RAWG API to allow users to search for new games, view details such as genres and platforms, and add titles directly to their backlog.
