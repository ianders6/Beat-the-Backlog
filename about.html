About Beat the Backlog

What This Project Is About

This website helps gamers organize their collection, track progress, and plan what to play next. It's designed to be a simple, efficient hub for managing your game library without the clutter.

How It Works

A simple, step-by-step layout showing how users can use key features:

  1. Sign up and log in to access your dashboard.
  2. Browse or add games to your library.
  3. View details and track progress.
  4. Personalize settings for your experience.
